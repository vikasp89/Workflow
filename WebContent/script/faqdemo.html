The Cost of Clear Aligners varies between Rs. 1 lac to Rs. 4.5 lacs (USD 1500 to USD 6000) all over the world. The various factors which control the prices of clear aligners are type and complexity of case, treating doctor, city and the clear aligner brand chosen.

Clear Aligners are the future of orthodontics. They are very effective in treating mild to moderate malocclusions and certain complex cases also by use of auxiliaries. Cases such as mild crowding, mild Spacing, crossbite and proclination can be easily treated using clear aligners.

The cost of Invisible braces in India depends on various factors, such as the severity of the patient's dental problem, Clear Aligner Brand, city e.g. Delhi, Mumbai, Lucknow, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Pune, Kerala, Bangalore, Chennai, Chandigarh, Gujarat etc. and the treating doctor. Clear or Invisible braces will roughly cost between 1- 4 lakhs in India.

Clear aligners or Invisible Braces as they call them can take anywhere from 6 months to 24 months.

With your Clear Aligners on you can only drink plain water but eating or drinking is not a limitation with clear aligners as is the case with fixed braces. You can remove your invisible braces, eat anything and everything that you like under the sun. After eating/drinking you can just clean your teeth and put your clear aligners back.

Medical insurance does not cover Clear Aligners treatment but don’t worry there are a lot of clear aligner companies which provide you with the option of easy EMI OR Finance. You can start your invisible braces treatment for as low as Rs 4999/-* per month.

Clear aligners or invisible braces don’t hurt as much as the fixed Metal or Ceramic Braces because the force they apply on your teeth to move them to the desired position is controlled unlike metal barces. They may be slightly uncomfortable for the first few days but once you get used to wearing your clear braces they are very comfortable and convenient.

Clear aligners are definitely better than metal braces in correcting mild to moderate orthodontic problems. The benefits of clear braces are many:-

  • They are invisible.

  • They are removable.

  • They apply controlled force over your teeth.

  • They help you maintain force over your dental hygiene properly.

  • There are no dietary restrictions with clear braces.

  • They do not cause ulcerations in your mouth like fixed braces.

Clear Aligners are the preferred choice of orthodontic mode of treatment by the new age orthodontist. With all the benefits they have over braces, they are the future.

Since clear aligners are removable and have controlled forces in built in them, they do not cause any side effects or discomfort. The same is not true for fixed braces, which leave white lesions on your teeth after they are removed on the completion of your treatment. Fixed braces also cause root resorption which is negligible with clear aligners.

After completing your treatment your treating doctor will give you retainers which are very similar to clear aligners. They have to be worn for a few months in order to prevent relapse. Based on your orthodontic correction your doctor may also choose to give you fixed retainers which are also not visible.
